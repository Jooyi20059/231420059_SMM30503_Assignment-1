My Manifesto

Welcome to My World

Hi, I'm JooYi, a visual storyteller and an aspiring freelance designer. I created Studio JooYi not only to showcase my work, but to build a space for free expression and personal evolution.

Here, I tell stories through visuals, express emotion through color, and convey ideas through layout. Every pixel, every piece of whitespace, is my way of speaking to the world.

Why Freelance Design?

Freelance doesn't mean aimless. It means I choose my direction. I want my work to have personality, warmth, and purpose. I want to choose who I work with, control my time, and grow in my own rhythm.

What is Studio JooYi?

A brand. A lab. A creative sanctuary. A beginning. It holds my dreams, learning, failure, passion, and hope. I'm a student striving to become a freelance designer. To me, design is not just an art form—it's a language of human connection.

My Vision

Freelancing gives me the space to create, explore different projects, manage my own time, and use design to influence the world. I believe in design that connects, communicates, and creates change.
