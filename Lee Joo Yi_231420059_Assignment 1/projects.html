Behind the Scenes

Ever wonder why I chose pastel green for Midori Café? It took five iterations—from geometric logos to abstract fonts. Inspiration hit after a matcha latte—soft, quiet, intentional. That's when the vision clicked: less noise, more space.

Each project represents a journey of exploration. The Bloom series emerged from personal healing, while DreamNotes was born from countless conversations with Gen Z creatives about their organizational struggles.
